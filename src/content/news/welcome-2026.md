We are excited to launch our new website! This platform will serve as the central hub for all Wayfarers Alumni activities, news, and connections.

## What You Can Do Here

- **Alumni Directory:** Search and connect with fellow Wayfarers from all graduation years
- **News & Updates:** Stay informed about upcoming events, reunions, and community news
- **Trip Reports:** Share and read about adventures from alumni around the world
- **Event Calendar:** Keep track of all upcoming alumni activities

## Getting Started

If you're a Wayfarers Hiking Society graduate, we encourage you to add your profile to our directory. Simply contact us with your details and we'll get you set up!

## Stay Connected

Don't forget to follow us on social media and join our email list to receive updates about upcoming events.

Here's to many more adventures together!
