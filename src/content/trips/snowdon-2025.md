## The Day

A group of eight Wayfarers alumni met at the Rhyd Ddu car park early morning, ready to tackle Snowdon via the PYG Track.

The weather was perfect - clear autumn skies with just enough cloud to create dramatic vistas without obscuring the views.

## The Climb

Starting at sea level, the group made steady progress up the well-defined path. The highlight came above 900m where the path opens up to reveal panoramic views across Snowdonia.

We reached the summit at 11:30 AM, just as the summit cafe was opening. Several members rewarded themselves with hot chocolate!

## The Descent

We descended via the Miner's Track, which offered different perspectives of the mountains and lakes below. The group arrived back at the cars by 3:30 PM.
