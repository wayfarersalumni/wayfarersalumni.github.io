We are thrilled to announce our Annual Alumni Reunion for 2026! This year's event will take place in the beautiful Peak District.

## Event Details

**Date:** Saturday, 14th June 2026  
**Location:** Bakewell, Derbyshire  
**Activity:** Guided hike through the Monsal Trail

## Schedule

- 10:00 AM - Meet at Bakewell train station
- 10:30 AM - Start of guided hike
- 1:00 PM - Picnic lunch at Monsal Dale
- 3:00 PM - Return to Bakewell
- 6:00 PM - Pub dinner (optional, book separately)

## What to Bring

- Sturdy walking boots
- Weather-appropriate clothing
- Packed lunch (or purchase on the way)
- Water and snacks

## Registration

Spaces are limited! Please register using our Google Form to confirm your attendance.

We look forward to seeing you there!
